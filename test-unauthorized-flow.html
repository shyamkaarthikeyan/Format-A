Testing Unauthorized Download Flow

Open the app at http://localhost:5000

Test Steps:

  1. Make sure you're NOT signed in (sign out if needed)
  2. Go to the editor/generator page
  3. Add a title and author to your document
  4. Look for download buttons - they should show "Sign in to Download" with lock icons
  5. Click a download button
  6. Verify the "Sign In Required" modal appears
  7. Click "Sign In" to test the redirect

Expected Behavior:

  • ✅ Download buttons show lock icons when not authenticated
  • ✅ Button text changes to "Sign in to Download Word/PDF"
  • ✅ Clicking download shows modal instead of attempting download
  • ✅ Modal has clear "Sign In Required" message
  • ✅ "Sign In" button redirects to /signin page
  • ✅ "Cancel" button closes the modal
